About Bits N' Grits

Bits N' Grits is a multichain NFT collection featuring 10,000 handcrafted, ai generative pixal art NFTs (Non Fungible Tokens). The name comes from the various 'grits' of bit, such as 8bit, 32bit, 64bit, and 128bit which will be found all throughout the collection.
